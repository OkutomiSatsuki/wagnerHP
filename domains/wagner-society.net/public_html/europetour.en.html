Wagner Society Orchestra of Keio University : 10th concert tour in Europe

日本語　Deutsch
Wagner Society Orchestra of Keio University is Japan's first non-professional student orchestra, consisting of 200 student members and led by professional conductors and trainers.
They will be conducting a brief European tour, performing in four countries in February and March 2014.
Each and every member is full of passion and very much looking forward to performing in front of audiences around Europe.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
1st concert: February 27 19:30 start/2nd concert:March 2 19:00 start
【Smetana Hall】(Praha/Czech)/【Hercules Saal】(Munchen/Germany)

Seikyo Kim, conductor
Dai Fujikura／Banitza Groove!（first performance of Czech/Germany）
Ravel／Antique Minuet
Stravinsky／Ballet suite "Fire Bird"（1919 version）
Berlioz／Symphony fantastic　op.14

3rd concert:March 6 19:30 start/4th concert:March 10 19:30 start
【Vienna Musikverein】(Vienna/Austria)/【Bartok Hall】(Budapest/Hungary)

Masahiko Okouchi, conductor
Beethoven／Leonore Overture No.3
Wagner／"Flying Dutchman" Overture
